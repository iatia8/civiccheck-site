✦ Civic Check
A voter-readiness thought tool

Get your vote ready.

Civic Check is a two-minute, nonpartisan iPhone app for registered voters. It surfaces the paperwork questions worth asking — name changes, ID rules, mail-ballot pitfalls, document deadlines — and points you to the official offices that can actually answer them.

It doesn't decide whether you can vote, and it isn't legal or election advice. Every state rule is dated and links to the official source first.

Support

Questions, corrections, or accessibility feedback are welcome — especially corrections. If a state rule shown in the app looks out of date, please say so and include your state.

American Indian and Alaska Native voters: call 866-OUR-VOTE and say so — you'll be connected with a Native American Rights Fund attorney (vote.narf.org).
