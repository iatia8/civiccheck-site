Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026

Civic Check does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Everything you enter — your state, your situation, how you plan to vote — stays on your device and is discarded when you start over or close the app. The app makes no network requests of its own; tapping an official link (such as your Secretary of State's website) opens it in your browser, where that site's own privacy policy applies. Sharing or printing your checklist uses Apple's standard share sheet, under your control.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to sell, share, breach, or subpoena. This is by design: thinking through your voting readiness is your business, not ours.
